# About Cat Dad Games

Cat Dad Games is a small collection of free, in-browser games. It is curated
and maintained by one person.

The aim is simple: a quiet place to find a good game, without ads, without
tracking, and without an account.

## Who this is for

The site is designed first for people who often have a harder time on the
modern web — older adults, people with low vision, people using a keyboard or
screen reader, and the staff at care centers, libraries, and classrooms who
help others get online.

If it works well for them, it works well for everyone.

## What you will not find here

- No ads.
- No third-party trackers.
- No account or sign-up.
- No data collection beyond simple, anonymous traffic counts.
- No autoplay video. No surprise sound.

## How games are chosen

Every game in this catalog is one I made or have personally tried. Each is
free to play, runs in a browser, and meets a baseline for accessibility.

The game itself opens in a new browser tab. Closing that tab brings you back
here. The site never embeds a game inside itself — it stays out of the way.

## Cost and hosting

The catalog is a plain, static website. Hosting is free on Cloudflare Pages.
There are no servers, no databases, and no per-visit costs. That is on
purpose — it means the site can stay up forever without depending on a
subscription or a sponsor.
