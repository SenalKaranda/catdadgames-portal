# About Cat Dad Games

Cat Dad Games is a small collection of free, in-browser games. It is developed
and maintained by [Senal Karanda](https://github.com/SenalKaranda).

## Who this is for

This site is built especially for older adults, nonprofits, and educational 
institutions seeking a collection of safe, free, and private games to play and share. 
I want it to be easy and enjoyable for anyone to use—whether you’re at home, 
working with a group, or helping others explore games online.

## What you will not find here

- **No** ads.
- **No** third-party trackers.
- **No** account or sign-up.
- **No** data collection beyond simple, anonymous traffic counts.
- **No** autoplay video. No surprise sound.

## How games are chosen

Every game in this catalog is one I've developed. Each is
free to play, runs in a browser, and meets a baseline for accessibility.
